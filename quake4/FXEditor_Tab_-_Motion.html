This tab controls the Velocity, Acceleration and Friction of particles. Remember that Up on an effect is relative to the Forward which is specified by the direction of the func_fx entity in a map, or by a bone placed in an .m5mesh.

Velocity – Forward

  • Speed to move particles in the forward axis.

Velocity – Left

  • Speed to move particles in the horizontal axis.

Velocity – Up

  • Speed to move particles in the vertical axis.

Acceleration – Gravity

  • The amount of gravity to apply to a segment.

Acceleration – Forward

  • Acceleration in the forward axis.

Acceleration – Left

  • Acceleration in the horizontal axis.

Acceleration – Up

  • Acceleration in the vertical axis.

Friction – Forward

  • Friction in both positive and negative numbers can be added to a segment. This affects friction in the forward axis.

Friction – Left

  • Friction in the horizontal axis.

Friction – Up

  • Friction in the vertical axis.
