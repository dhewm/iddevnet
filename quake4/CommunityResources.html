Quake 4 Community Resources

The following sites can be used as a general reference for various aspects of Quake 4. This list is in no way final or all-encompassing, so if you see something missing, would like to submit your own site under one of these categories, or if you feel something is incorrectly classified or described, please e-mail Andrew Weldon at aweldon@ravensoft.com.

  • Doom3World (Doom3 engine editing with dedicated Quake 4 sections)

  • LevelDesign.nl (General id engine editing community)

  • ModWiki (Doom3 engine editing)

  • Quake3Bits (Tips, tools, and tutorials)

  • Quake3World (Quake 3/4 discussion and level editing)

  • Quake4World (Wiki-style site with tutorials, map and mod links, as well as general game information)
