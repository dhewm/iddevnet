The Physics tab is used to apply impact physics to effects particles. Use sparingly as effects physics can be a performance killer.

None

  • The default, no physics on this particle.

Bounce on Impact

  • The sprite will bounce by the factor provided when it collides with a world object. Try small numbers from 0.2 -> 0.6 for best results.

Remove on Impact

  • This ensures that the sprite is removed from the world upon impact with a world object, rather than stay around for the entire duration of its lifetime. If collision does not happen by the end of a sprites lifetime, it will still disappear at the end of its life.
