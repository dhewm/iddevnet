The Electricity tab controls the lifetime of the electricity segment, the material used by the segment and whether it should play any effects upon itâ€™s lifetime ending. An electricity segment has all of the features of the Sprite and Line tabs plus:

Jitter - Table

  • The amount of jitter is controlled by a table.

Jitter - Forks

  • Adjusts the number of forks per electricity line spawned in the Emitter tab.

Jitter Rate

  • How much to ramp the random jitter over the lifetime of the particle.

Jitter Size

  • Controls how large to make the random jitter in the electricity segment.
