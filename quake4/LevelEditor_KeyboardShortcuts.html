Q4Radiant Keyboard Shortcuts/Tool Reference

This entire listing can also be found listed alphabetically by name within the Quake 4 LevelEditor from the Help Menu. Only shortcuts that have been verified as working properly are listed here. Most mouse-based shortcuts and commands are currently not listed and will be added with an upcoming update.

Most (but not all) standard Windows shortcuts (Ctrl+Z for Undo, Ctrl+O for Open, etc.) apply normally, and are not listed.
